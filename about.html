Our Mission

CMS is a web-based platform enabling students from various domains of study to submit complaints and receive fast responses within a fixed time frame from reliable sources.

This platform serves as a reliable tool for students to voice their complaints, ensuring further surveys and solutions to their academic problems. We believe in institutional transparency and empowering students to be active participants in their educational journey.
